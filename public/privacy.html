Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CyberBuddy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. WHAT WE COLLECT

  • Account data: Username, email address, hashed password
  • Usage data: Chat messages, quiz attempts, image scans
  • Payment data: Transaction references (we never store card numbers — Flutterwave handles this)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type (for security purposes)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To send payment receipts and important account emails
  • To detect fraud and abuse
  • To generate anonymous analytics

3. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Flutterwave — to process payments
  • Groq / Anthropic — to power AI features (your messages are processed but not stored by us long-term)

4. DATA RETENTION

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing us.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion, and opt out of non-essential emails.

6. SECURITY

We use bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and secure session management. However no system is 100% secure — please use a strong unique password.

7. COOKIES

We use session cookies only to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. CONTACT

For privacy concerns: privacy@cyberbuddy.app
